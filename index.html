Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to buy a property? +
The best time depends on your goals, but many buyers prefer spring for selection and autumn for better prices. Market trends and interest rates also matter.
How much should I budget for closing costs? +
Closing costs typically range from 2% to 5% of the purchase price. This includes lender fees, title insurance, taxes, and legal costs.
What’s the difference between pre-qualified and pre-approved? +
Pre-qualification is an estimate based on your finances. Pre-approval is a formal offer from a lender and carries more weight in real estate transactions.
